What fans are saying

December 30th 2024
Lovely venue and Florence was phenomenal! Considering she had just recovered from a broken foot, she could easily out run her security team-she’s like a little whippet. Her music touched my heart and always does. As I was busy singing my heart out and crying happy tears, I didn’t take any pictures or videos of the concert as I wanted to just absorb every moment and breathe it into every single cell in my body. Florence was incredible as always and let me tell you, when the Queen said “jump” I jumped my butt off! I loved every second of the concert. I’m just reading her book for the millionth time as I write this. I was just too excited for another concert so had to check online. I simply adore Florence and in November 2025 I’ll be walking down the aisle to one of her amazing songs. I just wish she could be there to hold my nervous hand down the isle.♥️ The whole experience of listening and watching and dancing with Florence Welch brings about a whole plethora of emotions but most prominently, love, healing and gratitude. 5 stars, I will be a flossy Fan until I die. Loved it.
